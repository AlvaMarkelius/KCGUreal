About Us

On average, King's has around 250–290 Graduate Students (MPhil, PhD, etc.). Graduate Students at King's come from all departments and backgrounds, and hail from all over the UK and the world. Grads and undergrads are both classified as Junior Members of the College and are said to be in statu pupillari (meaning you can't walk on the grass.) The King's graduate community is known for being friendly, diverse, welcoming, and active. There are lots of sports, societies, and academic opportunities at King's and at Cambridge, so we hope you make yourself at home here! In particular, the King's College Graduate Society, KCGS, hosts events, casual socials, themed formals, and much more throughout the year. To get involved in the grad community, check out these events, meet people in your accommodation, or hang around in the Graduate Suite (located on the first floor of A Staircase.) We look forward to getting to know you!.

King's Grad Bar & Grad Suite

King's Grad Bar

The King's Graduate Bar offers a relaxed, sociable atmosphere with a wide selection of drinks, comfortable seating, and regular events tailored for the graduate community.

Graduate Suite

The Grad Suite is a set of rooms in ‘A’ Staircase specifically for the graduate community. It includes a quiet study room, a computer room, and a common room with comfortable sofas, a foosball table, and a TV with surround sound, a Wii, and a PS4. We have an ever growing collection of films, boardgames, and books, as well as a small kitchen that usually has free tea and coffee. The graduate community uses the Grad Suite for social events and everyone is welcome to make themselves at home, though care should be taken not to disturb those working in the quiet study room. However, while we encourage you to use the Grad Suite, it cannot be reserved for an individual’s use; in particular it must not be used to host supervisions or other teaching.

Executive Committee Members

All graduate students at King’s are members of the King’s College Graduate Society! The Committee is elected annually by Society members, and we help to build and represent the community. KCGS Committee meetings are held once a fortnight and are open to everyone. Once a term we have a “General Meeting” where matters of importance to the whole graduate community are discussed, and all graduates are able to vote.
